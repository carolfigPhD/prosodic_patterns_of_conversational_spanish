Prosodic pattern found in dimension 3 low loadings


Description of dimension 3 low loadings

Many of the clips in Dimension 3 with low loadings contained pauses in the dialogue. These pauses occur when:

  • the speaker can't recall a word, express something in a single word, or express themselves
  • there has been a digression in the conversation and the speaker is trying to return to their storytelling or go back to their main point
  • the speaker gives a quick answer or description but then decides to elaborate
  • the speaker tries to conclude the topic or conversation
  • the speaker is thinking about a new topic, or starts a new topic
  • the speaker is expressing uncertainty or is unsure of how to continue the conversation

Prosodic observations:

  • Sometimes lengthening occurs before the pause and/or after the pause.
  • Along with lengthening, some words can also be creaky.
  • Creakiness seems to occur more often with male speakers.
