Prosodic pattern found in dimension 3 low loadings


Description of dimension 3 low loadings

Many of the clips in Dimension 3 seem to have turn-switches in the dialogue. The audios have been divided into three main categories where there are turn switches:

  • a speaker is agreeing with the interlocutor
  • a speaker is contributing to the conversation by continuing the topic (being a collaborator)
  • a speaker is trying to grab the floor
  • a speaker is answering a question or is asking a question
  • a speaker is backchanneling

It is not always the case that the speaker yields the floor, the turn switches in some of the audio samples oftentimes had overlapping speech.

Prosodic observations:

There were no consistent prosodic observations. Some of the speakers were creaky and had very reduced speech but it did not seem to be a consistent pattern.
