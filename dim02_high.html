Prosodic pattern found in dimension 2 high loadings


Description of dimension 2 high loadings

Many of the clips in Dimension 2 high loadings contained overlapping speech. The overlapping speech occurs when:

  • the speaker(s) laugh
  • a speaker is backchanneling or repeating the other interlocutor
  • a speaker asks a questions while the other speaker is speaking, or when a question is answered before the question ends
  • a speaker wants to contribute to the conversation by agreeing/supporting, sharing a similar experience, adding on to the conversation topic, trying to take over the turn or disagreeing

Main observations:

  • Many of the clips contained laughter.
  • Overall many of the clips had high intensity in the middle of the clip.
