Prosodic Patterns of Conversational Spanish



The Midlevel Prosodic Features Toolkit was used to analyze 12 conversations from the Dialogs Re-enacted Across Languages (DRAL) corpus. The toolkit applies Principal Component Analysis over prosodic features computed from dialogues. The toolkit outputs 12 dimensions which were then examined for prosodic patterns, each dimension may be an interpretable pattern.

The features used in the Midlevel toolkit are the following:

  • Hubert Redu: reduction predictions using HuBERT
  • Intensity
  • Pitch Height
  • Pitch Range
  • Lengthening
  • Creakiness
  • Disalignment: Peaks of intensity and pitch do not occur at the same time
  • CPPS: Cepstral Peak Prominence Smoothed
